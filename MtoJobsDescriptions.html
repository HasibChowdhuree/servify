Management Trainee Officer - MTO


Vacancy 1
Deadline 01 Jan, 2022
Salary Range BDT 25,000-45,000/month
Location Dhaka
Employment Status Full Time

Summary


This is a NIGHT SHIFT (6 PM - 3 AM) position. Please don't apply if you can't work during these hours. 

The MTO will be a part of the team that is responsible for optimal implementation of the company's overall activity plan and strategies. 

The incumbent will primarily be dealing with North American clients & stakeholders. So, a high proficiency in English communication skills is mandatory for the role.


Job Responsibilities


Incumbent in this role will primarily be responsible for, but not limited to:

  • End to end customer service.
  • Quality assurance of the services generated by the field force.
  • Ensuring timely service delivery in line of Servify Tech Solutions BD's brand promises.
  • Revenue generation from the clients.
  • Cross team coordination.

Educational Requirements


  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a reputed university. 
  • CGPA: 3 or above.
  • Combined CGPA in SSC & HSC: 9 or above.

Experience Requirements


  • Not mandatory, but nice to have.
  • Both fresh graduates and experienced professionals are encouraged to apply.

Additional Requirements


  • High proficiency in English communication (Speaking, Listening, Reading, Writing). 
  • High English typing speed & accuracy.
  • Curiosity & High learning propensity.
  • Computer literacy. 
  • Analytical capability. 
  • Leadership potential. 
  • Decision making capability. 
  • Ability to work with professionalism, discipline, & independence. 
  • Keen eye for details.
  • Ability to work under pressure & meet deadlines. 
  • Cross-cultural awareness. 
  • Customer-centricity & Service-mindset. 
  • Positive outlook & attitude. 
  • Empathy.
